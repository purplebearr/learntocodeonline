Maria Carrillo High School coders created a nonprofit to teach coding to young Sonoma County students at no cost

Our Commitment to Worldwide Education

At 404 Found Code, we believe that quality coding education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or background. We are constantly working to break down barriers and bring coding skills to learners around the globe.
